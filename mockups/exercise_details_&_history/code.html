Barbell Bench Press

Pectorals Compound Push
info Instructions
expand_more

Lie on your back on the bench with your eyes under the bar. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width. Unrack the bar, lower it to your mid-chest with control, and press it back up explosively to the starting position. Keep your feet planted and back arched slightly.
